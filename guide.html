Your Journey Guide

Follow these recommendations to enter and return from each experience with clarity and respect.

1. Preparation

Set clear intentions, create a safe space, and discuss medical history with your guide. Proper rest and a light diet are recommended before embarking.

2. During the Experience

Trust the process and remain open. Breathe deeply, stay hydrated, and allow emotions to flow without judgment. Your guide is there to support you.

3. Integration

After the journey, give yourself time to reflect. Journaling, gentle movement, and sharing insights with a supportive community help weave lessons into daily life.
