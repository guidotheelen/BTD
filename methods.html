Methods

Our approach blends time‑honored tradition with modern therapeutic practice. Each journey follows three core phases designed to foster insight and lasting transformation.

  1. Preparation – We help you clarify intentions, assess readiness, and create a safe container for exploration.
  2. Guided Journey – Skilled facilitators provide support and guidance throughout the psychedelic experience.
  3. Integration – After the journey we assist you in weaving insights into everyday life through reflection and community resources.
