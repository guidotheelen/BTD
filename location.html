Our Retreat Location

We host journeys in Nationaal Park De Groote Peel in the Netherlands.

De Groote Peel is one of the last raised bog landscapes in Western Europe. Once harvested for its peat, it is now a quiet reserve of wetlands, open water and heather fields. The tranquil boardwalk trails provide ideal space for contemplation and connection with nature.

Our retreat space sits on the park's edge, allowing easy access to the winding paths and bird-rich marshes. The stillness of this environment helps you integrate the work before and after each session.
