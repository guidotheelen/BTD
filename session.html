The Session Timeline

A glimpse at how a typical guided journey unfolds

1. Preparation (Weeks Prior)

Intention setting, health screening, and discussion of goals.

2. Arrival & Grounding

On the day of the session we gather, set the space, and review intentions.

3. Ingestion & Descent

The chosen plant medicine is taken with ceremony, guiding you inward.

4. Peak Experience

Several hours of deep exploration with the guide holding supportive presence.

5. Return

Gradual re‑entry to everyday awareness, sharing impressions and a light meal.

6. Integration

Follow‑up meetings in the days after the journey to anchor insights.
